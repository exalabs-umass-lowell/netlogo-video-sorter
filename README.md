# netlogo-vid-sorter
Temporary website for surveying autonomous drone swarm behaviors in simulations

Click [here](https://exalabs-umass-lowell.github.io/netlogo-video-sorter/) to take the survey!

Survey layout:
![Screen layout](/public/sample-layout-init.png)

## Instructions
1. Click on one of the two videos to select which is more complex.
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;**What is Complexity?** It is up to you to define!
2. Once you reach the ending screen, click **Submit** to send your results. 

## Notes
1. All results are anonymous and no personal information is collected.
2. This was produced in conjuction with **UMass Lowell Exalabs**, a research group dedicated to multiagent systems and emergent behavior in swarms.
